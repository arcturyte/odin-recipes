French Fries

Super Basic French Fries

There's nothing complicated about these french fries. We don't want to bother too much with it so we'll just get frozen fries from the store and put them in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Frozen French fries
  • Oven

Steps

  1. Well, open the bag, lay out the fries and put it in the oven for 12 minutes at 200C
  2. Your french fries are ready. But make sure to check crispyness and adjust to your liking!
