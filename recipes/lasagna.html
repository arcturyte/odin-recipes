Delicious Lasagna recipe

This lasagna goes back many centuries through time and space and is flavored by tears of the dead.

The recipe is pretty simple and we will get to it right after I tell you abou the time I did a lot of things that is completely irrelevant to this topic.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced meat
  • A lot of onions
  • Lots of different spices, but mostly salt and tomato sauce
  • Tons of cheese

Steps

  1. Mince the onions
  2. Cook the onions in onion, and add the meat
  3. Take the meat off the stove when it's cooked with some spices
  4. Prepare the oven and the baking stuff
  5. Once you've done all that, throw it all away and open your frozen dinner
